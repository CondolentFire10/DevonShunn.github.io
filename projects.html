Featured Project

Proffie Lightsaber

A fully custom cross-guard lightsaber built around the Proffie soundboard. Features hand-wired Neopixel blades, custom CFX sound fonts, and a 3D printed hilt designed to match screen-accurate proportions. The embedded control system handles motion-reactive lighting, clash detection, and audio playback in real time.
